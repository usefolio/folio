Folio is built for teams doing high-stakes document review: legal discovery, financial diligence, compliance, and regulated research. Use AI models to:

* Review and discard documents in bulk.
* Extract data from documents.

Most tools in this category (Legora’s Tabular Review, Harvey’s Review Tables and Hebbia’s Matrix) are SaaS-first. Folio is self-hosted and highly customizeable.

- Host in your own cloud.
- Keep sensitive files inside your environment.
- Use your own API keys for AI vendors.
- Customize to your needs.
- Avoid lock-in.

## AI-Powered document workspace for research and high-stakes knowledge work. 

Modern AI assistants blur the line between search and reasoning. Deep Research tools hide the process of gathering and vetting evidence and decide what to include without user input. In high-stakes knowledge work, where sources need to be curated carefully, this leads to unreliable outputs. Folio takes a different approach: users guide the search and curate documents before the AI reasoning step.

The leading AI-native business tools have all converged on tabular document review as a key offering: Legora's Tabular Review, Harvey's Review Tables, Hebbia's Matrix. All closed source, all managed services.

Folio delivers the same core capabilities (review at scale, integrated chat, reusable templates) as a source-available, self-hosted, extensible platform for internal use. You retain full control over your data and can customize the system to fit your workflow.
